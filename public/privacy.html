Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024

Overview

Harry's Budget Tracking is a personal finance app. Your data is private and only accessible to you.

Data We Collect

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for authentication and displaying your budget information. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Facial Recognition

Facial data is processed by FaceIO and is used only for login verification. It is not shared or used for any other purpose.
